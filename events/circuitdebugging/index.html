CIRCUIT DEBUGGING

  • Participants can compete individually or in teams of up to 4 members.
  • The event consists of multiple rounds, each increasing in difficulty.
  • Participants must debug and correct the given circuit within the specified time.
  • Only the provided components, tools, and simulators (if applicable) can be used.
  • External reference materials or mobile phones are not allowed.

EVALUATION CRITERIA:

  • Accuracy in identifying and fixing errors.
  • Time taken to debug the circuit.
  • Explanation of the identified errors and corrections.
