TECHNICAL QUIZ

  • Participants: 2 participants per team.
  • There will be a written preliminary round through which teams will be selected for the final round.
  • Finals will be in standard quizzing format which will be explained by the quiz master.
  • The specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to replay to a particular question and the type of round will be given before the start of the round.
  • Reporting time will be provided along with the scheduled of the event.
  • Decision of the organizers and the panel of judge(s)/quizmaster will be finalised.
