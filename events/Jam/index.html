JAM

  • Participants should speak on a given topic for one minute without hesitation, repetation or deviation from the chosen topic.
  • Grammatical errors, unnecessary pauses, jumbled words will be considered as errors and participants who point out any of these will be given a chance to speak next.
  • Participants should stay on the chosen topic, staying off-topic should be avoided.
  • A timer is set for one minute, and participants should aim to speak for the entire duration.
  • Participants should draw a chit randomly, the topic they draw will be the only one they speak about for a minute.
  • If a participant breaks a rule, they can be interrupted and it's next person turn.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no argument or appeal will be entertained.
