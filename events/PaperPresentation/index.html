PAPER PRESENTATION

  • Maximum number of participants in a team is 3-4.
  • The paper should be in IEEE document format and focus on technical topics.
  • The front page of paper should consist of participants/team members names, contact number, email id, college name and title at the top of the page.
  • Abstract should not exceed more than 250 words and paper should not exceed 15 pages.
  • All the participants are required to bring a hardcopy of their paper along with them on the day of event.
  • Kindly bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen-drive.
  • The teams will get 10 minutes for presentation and followed by QNA sessions.
  • Time limit should be strictly followed and extension may lead to deduction of marks.
